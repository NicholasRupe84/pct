Pre-Insulated distribution piping Farming complex

Click on picture to view larger image and description.

New straw and wood burning boiler producing 1,000,000 BTUs for hot water.

Buried pre-insulated piping installed for the distribution of the hot water to provide heating in the main farmhouse, out-buildings and a swimming pool.

The network of pre-insulated piping was pre-fabricated above ground and lowered into pre-excavated trenches.

All welded pipe joints were then encapsulated with field enclosures and insulation inserts for a complete continuous waterproof barrier.

4-inch carbon steel piping with an 8-inch HDPE outer casing provided the spine run to the building entry points.
