Indonesia

Dust Suppression systems at coal fired power station material handling operations. Dust suppression installed on 4 ship unloaders, 2 stacker-reclaimer machines and coal stockpile surface treatment.

The stockpile surface spray cannons were operated by a weather station to determine rainfall, humidity, and wind direction that sequenced partial spraying to avoid over-spray.

Water was taken from a large water storage basin that was also part of a project rainfall collection system during the monsoon periods.
