Travel

A specialist US based travel company offering a complete ‘door to door’ packaged service for Island locations.

Our growing itinerary currently includes our approved specialist agency status for the BAHAMAS – BRITISH VIRGIN ISLANDS – TAHITI – ENGLAND and IRELAND – THE ISLES OF SCILLY – THE CHANNEL ISLES

We are registered with NACTA (National Association of Career Travel Agents) and ARC (Airlines Reporting Corporation)as a Verified Travel Agency and operate as your approved representative of the Princess and Cunard cruise lines.

All our packaged island vacations are based on our knowledge of the location and our direct working association with vendors, suppliers and statutory organizations for the locations such as Tourist Boards and Chambers of Commerce etc. This allow us to provide information that is correct and up-to-date.
